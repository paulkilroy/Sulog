# Sulog — full curriculum plan

Target: **~10 words/phrases per lesson**, vocab-first then phrases within a unit.
Sources: existing app cards + Peace Corps course (grammar spine) + Tramp/Zorc
dictionary (verified spelling/stress). Sections = home tiles, in learning order.
"(new)" = not yet in the app (to author, dictionary-verified).

Rough scale: ~8 sections · ~34 units · ~45 lessons · ~450 cards.

---

## 1 · Survival Kit  — say something on day one
**1a Greetings & courtesy**
- L1 Hellos & thanks (10): Maupay nga aga · nga udto (new) · nga kulop · nga gab-i · Kumusta ka? · Maupay man · Salamat · Damo nga salamat · Pasensya na · Sige
- L2 Yes, no & getting by (10): Oo · Diri · Waray · Waray pa · Anay · Adi · Pwede · Hinay-hinay la · Pakpak anay · Sige, sunod na la

## 3 · Building Blocks — the grammar glue  *(mostly built)*
**3a Markers** (hi/ni/kan, an, hin/han, ha, ngan) — 2 lessons
**3b Demonstratives** (ini/iton/adto, hini/hiton/hadto, dinhi/didto) + nga — 2 lessons
**3c Possessives** (II: nakon/ko… ; III: akon/imo… + ha akon) — 2 lessons
**3d Asking** (ano/hin-o/hain/diin/san-o/kay ano/mapira/tagpira/ba + o) — 2 lessons
**3e Little words** (na/pa/liwat/gihapon/hin duro + waray/diri/Ayaw) — 2 lessons
**3f Verb tenses** (ma-/na-/-inm-: kaon, lakat, palit, inom) — 2 lessons
**3g Can, must, don't** (kinahanglan, pwede/mahimo, Ayaw) — 2 lessons
**3h "X is Y" & "the ___ is ___"** (equational + an/it; Amerikano ako, Marasa it adobo) — 2 lessons
