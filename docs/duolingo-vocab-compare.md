## Our Waray words NOT in Duolingo's first 162 (304)

_What we teach that Duolingo doesn't (yet) — by unit._

**Say hello & thanks:** Sige (Okay / go ahead) · Waray (None / nothing) · Anay (Wait / just a moment) · Adi (Here it is) · Pwede (Can / may (I)) · maupay (good) · tabang (help)

**Say who's who:** kita (we (inclusive)) · kami (we (exclusive)) · kamo (you / y'all) · hira (they)

**Name what you see:** hi (the (before a name)) · hin (a / some (object)) · han (of / the (past object)) · saging (banana)

**This & that:** iton (that (near you)) · adto (that (over there)) · kahoy (tree)

**Ask what, where, who:** hin-o (who) · ano (what) · diin (where (from / which)) · mapira (how many) · ba (makes a yes/no question)

**Count to 100:** upat (four) · lima (five) · unom (six) · pito (seven) · walo (eight) · siyam (nine) · napulo (ten) · karuhaan (twenty)

**Introduce your family:** tatay (father) · nanay (mother) · anak (child) · apoy (grandparent) · apo (grandchild) · bugto (sibling) · lalaki (man) · babaye (woman) · bata (child) · ulitawo (young man) · daragita (young woman) · lagas (old person) · kaharani (neighbor) · bisita (visitor) · tawo (person) · miyembro (member)

**Say what's yours:** niya (his / her) · naton (our (incl)) · namon (our (excl)) · nira (their)

**Say it's mine:** iya (his / hers) · aton (ours (incl)) · amon (ours (excl)) · iyo (yours (pl)) · ira (theirs)

**Say “X is Y”:** an (the (completed / past)) · mabaho (smelly)

**Already, not yet, just:** na (already) · pa (still / yet)

**Do it: now / will / did:** makaon (will eat) · nakaon (is eating) · kinmaon (ate) · malakat (will go) · nalakat (is going) · linmakat (went) · mapalit (will buy) · napalit (is buying) · pinmalit (bought) · mainom (will drink) · nainom (is drinking) · inminom (drank)

**Talk about work:** estudyante (student) · turista (tourist) · nars (nurse) · panday (carpenter) · pastor (pastor) · misyonaryo (missionary) · abugado (lawyer) · mangingisda (fisherman) · mag-uroma (farmer) · direktor (director) · kapitan (captain) · mayor (mayor / boss) · konsehal (councilman) · negosyante (businessman) · sekretarya (secretary) · hardinero (gardener) · tag-iya (owner) · ninong (godfather) · pasyente (patient)
