An Adlaw ni Nanay — Mom's Day
Alas singko, sayu bumangon hi Nanay. Nagluto hiya kape ngan kan-on para ha pamilya.
At five o'clock, Mom woke up early. She cooked coffee and rice for the family.
Pagkatapos, nagpasyada hiya ha natad. Limpyo an ira balay pirmi.
Afterwards, she strolled in the yard. Their house is always clean.
Buwas, magkita hira ni Tatay ha merkado para magpalit isda.
Tomorrow, she and Dad will meet at the market to buy fish.
